# Team Project

This is your team's project repository. Your full development environment runs in the cloud
via **GitHub Codespaces** — no setup needed on your laptop.

## Getting started

1. Click the green **`< > Code`** button → **Codespaces** → **Create codespace on main**.
2. Wait ~90 seconds for it to build. Claude Code, Node, and Git are installed automatically.
3. Follow the **Day-1 setup guide** your team lead shared with you.

## Working day to day

- Make changes, then use the **Source Control** panel → **Commit & Sync** to save your work
  (commits and pushes in one click — no terminal needed).
- Ask **Claude Code** in the terminal (`claude`) for help building and debugging.

Questions? Ask your team lead — asking early beats guessing.
